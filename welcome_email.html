Welcome note

You made it. You’re not alone.

Hey — thanks for finding Sanity & Secrets.

This space was made for people who’ve been left out, let down, or lied to by the stories they were handed.

You can expect posts that feel personal, reflective, angry, curious, and human. Sometimes it’ll be a survival note. Sometimes a history thread. Sometimes just a truth that needed somewhere to land.

You don’t have to agree with everything I write.
You just have to show up with curiosity, and maybe a little fire.

The Blog Bog is open.
And if no one else told you this today: you’re not too much. You’re not too weird. And you’re definitely not alone.

— George Turtle
