Welcome note

You made it. You’re not alone.

Hey — thanks for finding Sanity & Secrets.

This space was made for people like us — people who’ve been left out, let down, or lied to.

You can expect one message a week. Sometimes it’ll be a survival guide. Sometimes a rant. Sometimes a truth history won’t teach. But always honest. Always human.

You don’t have to agree with everything I write.
You just have to show up with curiosity, and maybe a little fire.

The first piece is already here.
And if no one else told you this today: you’re not too much. You’re not too weird. And you’re definitely not alone.

Let’s burn the old stories down and build something better.

— George Turtle
