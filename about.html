Welcome to Sanity & Secrets

Unfiltered truths about history, belief, and surviving the madness.

I write for the people who were lied to by history books, let down by religion, gaslit by society, and told their feelings were a disorder.

Each issue, I unravel one thread: a suppressed truth, a forgotten story, or a raw survival insight. I talk about mental health without sugarcoating it, historical atrocities that still shape how we suffer, and the weird, wonderful power of nerd culture to keep us alive.

From the Younger Dryas to the Vatican, from ADHD to Lord of the Rings — nothing’s off-limits.

You’re not broken. You’ve just been surviving inside a system of secrets. And I’m here to expose them — one truth at a time.
