About Lord G.S.T

I’m a multidisciplinary creator from Bristol and a developer of persistent environments — systems that do more than display information and instead hold tone, memory, and pressure over time.

My work doesn’t sit neatly in one category. It moves across writing, research, systems design, dark narrative architecture, worldbuilding, philosophy, survival, theology, and the hidden social machinery people are expected not to notice.

This space exists because I’ve spent too long feeling the friction of being a neurodivergent person in a world built to flatten anything that doesn’t move in the approved rhythm. So I built something with its own rhythm instead.

Sanity & Secrets is not just a publication. It’s a curated environment: part archive, part study, part confession booth, part engine room. A place where polished research can sit beside raw reflection without either one cancelling the other out.

If you’re here, you’re entering a space shaped by systems-thinking, dark curiosity, and the belief that the truth usually lives somewhere beneath the surface layer.
