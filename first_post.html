You’re Not Lazy, You’re Tired of Bullshit

An opening statement for everyone who has ever been called lazy when what they really were was overloaded, exhausted, and forced to survive inside the wrong system.

Let’s get this out the way: you’re not lazy.

You’re burnt out.
You’re overstimulated and under-supported.
You’re grieving a world that gaslights your wiring and mocks your pain.

Mental health isn’t about being happy. It’s about making sense of a system that demands obedience and calls it sanity.

Some days, the win is getting out of bed.
Other days, it’s not punching a hole through your own timeline.

You’re not broken. You’re adapting. And if no one else says it today: I’m glad you made it.

This publication is for you.
Let’s crack it open.
