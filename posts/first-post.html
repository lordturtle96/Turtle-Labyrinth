Why I Made This

This site exists because I needed somewhere that could hold honesty without flattening it into a brand.

I didn’t want to build another polished corner of the internet that sounded like everyone else. I wanted somewhere I could write as a person — not as a product, not as a content machine, and not as someone pretending to have everything figured out.

Sanity & Secrets is where I want to talk about the things that keep returning: mental health, history, grief, belief, anger, strange wonder, survival, and the stories people use to keep going when the official versions stop making sense.

Some of this will be deeply personal. Some of it will be historical. Some of it will be theological, messy, nerdy, or quietly furious. Most of it will probably be all of those things at once.

I made this because I know what it feels like to need language for something and not find it anywhere. To feel too much, think too much, notice too much, and still be told the problem is you.

This site is my answer to that.

Not a perfect answer. Not a finished answer. But a real one.

If you’re here, welcome. I hope some of these words help you feel less alone, less broken, and more able to recognise your own mind as something worth listening to.

— George Turtle
